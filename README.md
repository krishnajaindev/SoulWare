# 🧠 SoulWare

**SoulWare** is an AI-powered **mental health and wellness platform** designed for **students in higher education institutions**.  
It provides **accessible, anonymous, and continuous mental health support**, addressing stress, anxiety, and emotional challenges while reducing the stigma around seeking help.

## 🌟 Overview

SoulWare connects **students, counselors, and institutions** through a unified, privacy-focused platform.  
It offers a multi-level mental health ecosystem combining **AI-driven assistance**, **peer community interaction**, and **professional counseling** — ensuring emotional well-being and holistic growth.

The platform promotes inclusivity through **multi-language accessibility** and culturally sensitive self-care tools, enabling students from diverse backgrounds to find support in their comfort zone.

---

## 💡 Key Features

### 🤖 AI Chatbot (Powered by Chatbase)
- Provides **24/7 conversational support** to students.
- Detects emotional cues and suggests self-help resources.
- Built using **Chatbase AI** for reliable, context-aware, and safe interactions.

### 🕵️ Anonymous Counseling
- Students can connect with certified **mental health professionals** without revealing their identity.
- Helps eliminate stigma and fear of judgment while seeking help.

### 💬 Peer-to-Peer Community
- A moderated, community-driven space where students can share experiences and support one another.
- Volunteers help maintain a safe and empathetic environment.

### 🌍 Multi-Language Support
- Integrated **Google Translator API** for real-time translation of chats and resources.
- Ensures accessibility for students across linguistic backgrounds.

### 🧘 Interactive Wellness Modules
- Scientifically designed **quizzes, exercises, and meditation tools**.
- Includes **calming games and soothing music** to encourage mindfulness and focus.

### 📊 Admin & Counselor Dashboards
- **Admins:** Manage staff, monitor engagement, and view analytics.  
- **Counselors:** Manage appointments, chat with students, and track progress securely.
